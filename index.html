Welcome to War History Online - Your online learning starts here

At War History Online, we understand the importance of concise and accessible information for students, scholars, and history enthusiasts alike.


We are your go-to online resource for a comprehensive and vast array of extensively, organised and carefully curated historical notes.


We look at some of the key campaigns that helped shape the outcomes of battle and change the course of history for the better. We will look at those that influenced decisions, from well known Generals to politicians.


We take a look at sacrifices made by animals and the important roles they played in some of the bloodiest and horrific moments of war.


Our extensive and carefully curated notes all come from talks given by experts in their field. Each expert is credited on the page, and we feature a further reading section, so if you would like to continue to learn about and further explore the topic or era covered you can be sure to do so.


So what are you waiting for?


Go ahead and immerse yourself in our carefully compiled and structured, interesting and well-organised content, which captures the essence of the historical events and learn from the triumphs and tragedies of the past.
